What are Leased Lines?

A leased line is a dedicated, fixed-bandwidth internet connection that offers symmetric speeds and uncontended performance between locations.

Key Characteristics

  • Dedicated private connection
  • Symmetric speeds
  • SLA-backed uptime
  • 24/7 monitoring
